# SCOPE

## Goal

Title (working): _e.g. "SPEAR: A Five-Phase Framework for AI That Actually Completes Tasks"_

Single-thesis sentence: _the one idea you want a reader to take away_.

## Audience

Who's reading? What problem are they trying to solve?

## Inputs

- [ ] Outline / notes: `path/to/notes.md`
- [ ] Reference posts to link
- [ ] Quotes / data sources

## Constraints

- **Length target:** _e.g. 1500-2000 words_
- **Image cadence:** one image every 400-600 words
- **Voice:** practitioner-direct, first person, no hedging

## Done means

- [ ] Single thesis defended throughout
- [ ] Lead anecdote opens the post
- [ ] Closing echoes the title
- [ ] Every assertion sourced or framed as opinion
- [ ] Headers consistent (title-case OR sentence-case throughout)
