# ASSESS — Blog

## Scored metrics

| # | Metric | Mechanical | What |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Single thesis | no | Defended throughout. |
| 2 | Lead anecdote | no | Opens with a story / data point. |
| 3 | Pyramid (per section) | no | First sentence of each section IS its punchline. |
| 4 | Voice consistency | no | Matches SCOPE.md throughout. |
| 5 | Source discipline | no | Every assertion cited or framed as opinion. |
| 6 | Structure | no | Outline followed; each section earns its place. |
| 7 | Tightness | no | No filler. |
| 8 | Image cadence | yes | One image per ~600 words. |
| 9 | Closing echoes title | no | Last paragraph references the title. |
| 10 | Ship-readiness | yes | Headers consistent, links work. |

## Lettered failure modes

A. **Buried lead** — actual hook isn't in the first 50 words.
B. **Multiple theses** — paragraph N introduces a second main idea.
C. **Academic hedging** — "it could be argued…", "some might say…".
D. **Generic VC speak** — buzzwords without numbers.
E. **Jargon clustering** — three+ industry terms without explanation.
F. **Filler sentences** — sentences deletable without meaning loss.
G. **Header case mixing** — title-case + sentence-case mixed.
H. **Quote not earned** — pulled quote without setup.
I. **Closer disconnected** — closing doesn't echo the title.
J. **Paragraph length** — paragraph >5 sentences.
K. **Wall of text** — section >400 words with no break.

## Convergence

PASS when every section is 10/10 across all 10 metrics AND no failure modes hit.
